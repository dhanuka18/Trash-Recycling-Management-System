About Feedback

Your feedback helps us improve our recycling management system and provide better service to our users. We value your input and use it to make informed decisions about future updates and improvements.

What to Include

  • Clear description of your feedback
  • Steps to reproduce (for bugs)
  • Expected vs actual behavior
  • Screenshots if applicable

Response Time

We typically respond to feedback within 2-3 business days. For urgent issues, please contact our support team directly.
